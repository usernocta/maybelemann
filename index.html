About Symbio

Symbio is a multi‑model AI system designed to merge insights from different neural networks into one refined, verified conclusion. It balances creativity, logic, and precision — allowing users to receive responses that feel alive, reasoned, and reliable.

Core Features

Dual Intelligence Layers

Free Core: Gemini and DeepSeek for fast and accessible reasoning.
Premium Core: ChatGPT and Grok for deep analytical and creative outputs.

Smart Arbiter

Symbio’s internal arbiter compares and weights answers from all models, choosing the most coherent, evidence‑based response.

Privacy‑First

All data is processed anonymously. Keys stay server‑side, and no personal information is stored or shared.

FAQ

How does the arbiter decide which answer wins?
It evaluates logical consistency, confidence level, and alignment across model opinions — producing a unified, weighted conclusion.
Will Symbio stay free?
Yes, the Gemini + DeepSeek tier remains free. Premium access (ChatGPT + Grok) will expand with additional features later.
